About Texas Shark Tank

TST is a one-of-a-kind opportunity for entrepreneurs (undergraduate and graduate) in Texas to pitch their startups to a panel of angel investors, venture capitalists, and serial entrepreneurs in Austin. Students are able to get the full force of Austin’s entrepreneurship community all in one place.


We welcome startups of all stages, whether you're just building out your first business plan, testing a prototype, or are already making sales. Competitors will get the chance to refine their businesses by executing implementation strategies, receiving technical support, and designing product mock-ups & prototypes before having the chance to pitch investors for funding.


Past participants have walked away with offers valued at hundreds of thousands of dollars in funding, so don’t wait, get your startup signed up today!

Research

Determine if your idea is viable by analyzing the market, the needs of the target audience, costs and benefits, and competition.

Mock

Leverage Texas Convergent's interdisciplinary talent and group structure to build technical mockups for your team's business model.

Pitch

During the event, get advice from industry experts, talent networking opportunities, and potential seed funding from investors.

Competition Details

Register your team here before April 1st, 2019 at 11:59PM for a chance to compete in Texas Shark Tank. After applications close, a selected panel of judges and mentors will review the applications and invite the top 24 teams to pitch the day of the event.



In order to be presented at Texas Shark Tank, your startup/idea must meet the following requirements:
  • The team is composed of students (undergraduate or graduate) of any major currently enrolled full-time at a United States university.
  • Mentors and/or advisors on the startup team can help with preparation of the pitch but may not participate in the pitch itself.
  • The startup must be pre-funding (this means you have not raised dilutive capital from outside investors).
