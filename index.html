About Texas Shark Tank

TST is a one-of-a-kind opportunity for undergraduate entrepreneurs at UT to pitch their startups to a panel of angel investors, venture capitalists, and serial entrepreneurs in Austin. Students are able to get the full force of Austin’s entrepreneurship community all in one place.


We welcome startups of all stages, whether you're just building out your first business plan, testing a prototype, or are already making sales. Competitors will get the chance to refine their businesses by executing implementation strategies, receiving technical support, and designing product mock-ups & prototypes before having the chance to pitch investors for funding.


Past participants have walked away with offers valued at hundreds of thousands of dollars in funding, so don’t wait, get your startup signed up today!

Research

Determine if your idea is viable by analyzing the market, the needs of the target audience, costs and benefits, and competition.

Mock

Leverage Texas Convergent's interdisciplinary talent and group structure to build technical mockups for your team's business model.

Pitch

During the event, get advice from industry experts, talent networking opportunities, and potential seed funding from investors.

About USIT

The University Securities Investment Team is the premier investing organization at The University of Texas at Austin. Each year, it hosts three state-wide competitions: Texas Charity Pitch, Texas Stock Pitch, and Texas Shark Tank. Learn more by visiting our website at texasusit.org.
